About Me

Hello, I'm
Navdeep

I am an 18-year-old girl from the historic city of Sri Chamkaur Sahib, Punjab — a land of courage, sacrifice, and deep spiritual heritage.

I have completed my senior secondary education from Govt. Eminence of Girls Sen. Sec. School, Sri Chamkaur Sahib. My school has shaped my personality and given me the foundation to dream big.
